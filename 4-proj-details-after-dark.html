Art After Hours Branding

Design Hub, a club at Central Michigan University I serve on the E-Board of was chosen to put on an event for Fire Up All Night, a lock-in style event where clubs put on activities late at night. We were given $2,000 to put on the event of our dreams, and I was able to design the posters, Instagram posts, and t-shirts for the event.
The Design Hub logo was designed by club president Ava Zaffarano.
