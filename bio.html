VERONICA LESSING

flex wrap

Hello! My name is Veronica Lessing, and I am an artist, graphic designer, and student at Central Michigan University. I currently work at Media Graphix, a division of Central Michigan University Communications. I work with clients such as CMU Admissions, where I design various types of collateral to be sent to incoming and prospective students. I have done several wide format projects with the College of Science and Engineering and the College of Liberal Arts and Social Studies
at Central Michigan University.

In my free time, I love to listen to music and hang out with my roommates Cole and Sawyer, and our cat Franklin. As a student at CMU, I am in pursuit of a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree with a concentration in Graphic Design and a minor in Art History. I also specialize in printmaking in my free time.
