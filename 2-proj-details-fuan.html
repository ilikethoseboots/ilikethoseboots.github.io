FIRE UP ALL NIGHT SPIRIT MARK

These projects were for the College of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences (CLASS) at Central Michigan University, in Anspach Hall. The CLASS Student Hub wall wrap was for a room students in the college could use for homework and other activities. Similarly, the quiet room and collaboration room door wraps denoted rooms for CLASS students to use. My coworker, Helena Schneider, designed the logo for the CLASS Student Hub, and I matched her design elements to create the wrap for the student hub itself.

Thank you for visiting!
